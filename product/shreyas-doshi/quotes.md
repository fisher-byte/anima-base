# Shreyas Doshi 经典语录集

> 产品管理思想家 Shreyas Doshi 的智慧结晶

---

## 🎯 时间管理

### LNO Framework

> "Not all tasks are created equal. The key is knowing which tasks deserve your best effort and which just need to get done."

不是所有任务都一样。关键是知道哪些任务值得你全力以赴，哪些只需要完成即可。

---

> "The LNO framework changed my life. Once you see tasks through this lens, you can't unsee it."

LNO框架改变了我的人生。一旦你通过这个视角看任务，就再也无法回到从前。

---

> "Most people burn out not because they work too much, but because they're spending way too much energy on N and O tasks."

大多数人疲惫不是因为工作太多，而是因为在N和O任务上花了太多精力。

---

### 日历管理

> "Your calendar is your portfolio. Invest your time where it gives the highest returns."

你的日历就是你的投资组合。把时间投资在回报最高的地方。

---

> "If you don't control your calendar, your calendar will control you."

如果你不控制你的日历，你的日历就会控制你。

---

> "Calendar Theater: When your calendar looks impressively full, but your output is surprisingly empty."

日历剧场：当你的日历看起来很满，但你的产出却出奇地空。

---

## 🚀 策略与执行

> "Most execution problems are actually strategy problems in disguise."

大多数执行问题实际上是伪装的策略问题。

---

> "Before you try to fix execution, make sure you don't have a strategy problem."

在尝试修复执行之前，确保你没有策略问题。

---

> "If your team is struggling with execution, first ask: have I provided clear strategy?"

如果你的团队在执行上挣扎，首先问：我是否提供了清晰的策略？

---

## 💡 机会成本思维

> "ROI thinking asks: Is this worth doing? Opportunity cost thinking asks: Is this the BEST thing I could be doing?"

ROI思维问：这值得做吗？机会成本思维问：这是我能做的最好的事吗？

---

> "Every time you say yes to something, you're saying no to something else. Make sure you're saying no to the right things."

每次你对某事说"是"，你就对另一件事说"不"。确保你对正确的事说"不"。

---

## ⚡ High Agency

> "High Agency is about finding a way to get what you want, without waiting for conditions to be perfect or otherwise blaming the circumstances."

High Agency是关于找到方法得到你想要的，而不是等待完美条件或责怪环境。

---

> "High Agency people either push through in the face of adverse conditions or manage to reverse the adverse conditions to achieve goals."

High Agency的人要么在逆境中突破，要么设法逆转不利条件来实现目标。

---

> "When hiring, I prefer Go Getters over Frustrated Geniuses."

招聘时，我更喜欢Go Getters而非Frustrated Geniuses。

---

> "Never compromise on Integrity, not even for Game Changers."

永远不要在诚信上妥协，即使对于Game Changers也不行。

---

## 📊 三层产品工作

> "There are 3 levels of product work: 1) Impact 2) Execution 3) Optics. Each is important. AND Everyone has a default level they enjoy."

产品工作有三个层次：1) 影响 2) 执行 3) 形象。每个都重要。每个人都有自己默认喜欢的层次。

---

> "If you only focus on Impact and Execution but ignore Optics, your great work might go unnoticed and unrewarded."

如果你只关注Impact和Execution而忽视Optics，你的出色工作可能不会被注意到也不会被奖励。

---

## 🛡️ Pre-mortem

> "The best way to deal with problems is to prevent them from happening in the first place."

处理问题最好的方式是首先防止它们发生。

---

> "Pre-mortems are one of the most powerful tools I've seen for preventing problems before they happen."

Pre-mortem是我见过的在问题发生前预防它们的最强大工具之一。

---

> "Most teams are good at post-mortems. Very few are good at pre-mortems. That's a massive missed opportunity."

大多数团队擅长post-mortem。很少有团队擅长pre-mortem。这是一个巨大的错失机会。

---

## 👔 领导力

> "3 types of product leaders: 1) The Operator 2) The Craftsperson 3) The Visionary."

三种产品领导者：1) 运营者 2) 工匠 3) 远见者

---

> "Most product leaders are naturally strong in one archetype, but the best ones develop capabilities in at least two."

大多数产品领导者在一种类型上天生强大，但最好的那些会在至少两种类型上发展能力。

---

## 🎭 职业发展

> "The best product managers I know are ruthless about their time. They say no to most things so they can say yes to the right things."

我认识的最好的产品经理对自己的时间很严格。他们对大多数事说不，这样他们才能对正确的事说是。

---

> "Your career is a portfolio of experiences. Diversify wisely."

你的职业是经验的投资组合。明智地多元化。

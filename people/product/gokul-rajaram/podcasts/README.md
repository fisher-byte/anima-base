## 💡 核心洞察汇总

### 关于产品管理
> "Product managers are reducers - they reduce complexity for users and teams"

> "The best PMs are the ones who make everyone around them better"

### 关于决策
> "The SPADE framework isn't about making perfect decisions - it's about making transparent, traceable decisions that you can learn from"

### 关于速度
> "Speed is a feature. Users notice when your product is fast, and they definitely notice when it's slow"

### 关于团队
> "Small teams with end-to-end ownership will always beat large teams with dependencies"

### 关于AI时代
> "Experience can be a liability in the AI era. What worked in the past five years may not work in the next five months"

> "The old playbooks for product and go-to-market no longer work in the AI era"

### 关于创业
> "Don't start with 'I want to build a company.' Start with 'I want to solve this problem'"

> "Durability genetics matter more than vanity metrics like '0 to $2M in 10 days'"

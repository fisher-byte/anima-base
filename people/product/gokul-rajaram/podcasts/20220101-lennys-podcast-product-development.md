# Gokul Rajaram on product development, hiring, and angel investing

## Key Insights

### Getting into Tech
- Joined Google in 2003 (second choice after Cisco rescinded offer)
- "Literally saved from yourself by circumstance"
- Started in syndication (distributing Google search/ads to partners like AOL, Yahoo)

### Finding Product Opportunities
- Walked around Google office at 6 PM
- Found engineers working on a project from Sergey Brin
- Project: Reverse Google's search algorithm to extract keywords from webpages for ad targeting
- Became their part-time PM (nights and weekends)
- Within 3 months, project became Google AdSense

### Current Roles
- **DoorDash**: Executive team, leads Caviar product line
- **Board Member**: Coinbase, Pinterest, The Trade Desk (all public companies)
- **Angel Investor**: Prolific investor in startups

### Philosophy
- Calls himself "startup helper"
- Makes time to mentor founders
- Known for being humble despite impressive resume

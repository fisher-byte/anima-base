# Marty Cagan 经典语录集

> SVPG创始人 Marty Cagan 的智慧结晶

---

## 🎯 产品管理核心

### 产品经理的本质

> "The job of the product manager is to discover a product that is valuable, usable, feasible, and viable."

产品经理的工作是发现有价值、可用、可行、商业上可持续的产品。

---

> "If you're just using your engineers to code, you're only getting about half their value."

如果你只是让工程师写代码，你只获得了他们一半的价值。

---

> "The best single indicator of a strong product manager is their command of the user and customer."

判断一个优秀产品经理最好的指标是他对用户和客户的深入理解。

---

> "If you're not talking to users every single week, you're probably not doing product management."

如果你不是每周都在和用户交流，你很可能不是在做产品管理。

---

### 产品发现

> "The purpose of product discovery is to make sure we have some evidence that when we ask the engineers to build a production-quality product, it won't be a waste of time and money."

产品发现的目的是确保当我们要求工程师构建生产级产品时，不会浪费时间和金钱。

---

> "Our higher order objective is to validate our ideas the fastest, cheapest way possible."

我们的首要目标是以最快、最便宜的方式验证我们的想法。

---

> "It doesn't matter how good your engineering team is if they are not given something worthwhile to build."

如果工程团队没有被给予值得构建的东西，他们有多优秀都没有意义。

---

## 👥 产品团队

### 赋能 vs 功能

> "We need teams of missionaries, not teams of mercenaries."
> — 引用John Doerr

我们需要传教士团队，而非雇佣兵团队。

---

> "In a feature team, the engineers are there to implement. In a product team, the engineers are there to solve problems."

在功能团队中，工程师是来实现的。在产品团队中，工程师是来解决问题的。

---

> "Teams of missionaries are engaged, motivated, have a deep understanding of the business context, and tangible empathy for the customer."

传教士团队是投入的、有动力的，深刻理解业务背景，对客户有真实的共情。

---

> "It's nearly impossible to have a team of missionaries when your engineers work for another company and are under contract to build what you tell them to."

当你的工程师为另一家公司工作并且按合同构建你告诉他们的东西时，几乎不可能拥有传教士团队。

---

### 产品管理剧场

> "Most product managers are essentially project managers with a different title."

大多数产品经理本质上只是换了头衔的项目经理。

---

> "The sad reality is that at most companies, the vast majority, the people called product managers are not actually doing product management."

悲伤的现实是，在大多数公司，被称为产品经理的人实际上并没有在做产品管理。

---

## 🎯 产品战略

### 愿景与战略

> "The product vision describes the future you are trying to create, and most importantly, how that future improves the lives of your customers."

产品愿景描述你试图创造的未来，更重要的是，这个未来如何改善客户的生活。

---

> "Strategy is about making hard choices. If everyone agrees with your strategy, you probably haven't made any real choices."

战略是关于做出艰难选择。如果每个人都同意你的战略，你可能没有做出任何真正的选择。

---

> "Strong product teams need to understand the context in which they are working. This means they need to understand the product vision and the product strategy."

强大的产品团队需要理解他们工作的背景。这意味着他们需要理解产品愿景和产品战略。

---

## 🚀 转型与变革

### 产品运营模型

> "Transformation is not about adopting a process. It's about changing how you think about technology's role in your business."

转型不是采用一个流程。它是关于改变你对技术在业务中角色的思考方式。

---

> "The product operating model is what distinguishes the best product companies from the rest."

产品运营模型是区分最好的产品公司与其他公司的关键。

---

> "Most companies are still operating as if technology is a cost center rather than a competitive advantage."

大多数公司仍然在运营时把技术当作成本中心，而非竞争优势。

---

## 👔 产品领导力

### 领导者角色

> "The role of the product leader is not to make the product decisions. It's to create an environment where the teams can discover and deliver products customers love."

产品领导者的角色不是做产品决策。而是创造一个环境，让团队能够发现和交付客户喜爱的产品。

---

> "Your job is to develop your people into great product people. If you're not actively coaching your people, you're not doing your job."

你的工作是把你的人培养成优秀的产品人。如果你不在积极地教练你的人，你就没有在做你的工作。

---

> "One of the most impactful things a product leader can do is to provide compelling product vision."

产品领导者能做的最有影响力的事情之一是提供有说服力的产品愿景。

---

## 📊 成果导向

### OKR与成果

> "OKRs should be about outcomes, not outputs."

OKR应该是关于成果，而非产出。

---

> "The key result is the outcome you're trying to achieve, not the output. Building a feature is not a key result – that's an output. The impact of the feature is the key result."

关键结果是你试图实现的成果，而非产出。构建一个功能不是关键结果——那是产出。功能的影响才是关键结果。

---

> "OKRs are what allow teams to move from being given features to build, to being given problems to solve."

OKR让团队从被给予功能去构建，转变为被给予问题去解决。

---

## 💡 创新与风险

### 四大风险

> "Product discovery is about answering four key questions: Will the user buy it? Can the user figure out how to use it? Can we build it? Does it work for our business?"

产品发现是关于回答四个关键问题：用户会买吗？用户能弄清楚怎么用吗？我们能构建吗？对我们的业务可行吗？

---

> "The Product Manager is responsible for the value and viability risks, and overall accountable for the product's outcomes."

产品经理负责价值和可行性风险，并对产品的成果负总体责任。

---

## 🔥 行业洞察

### AI时代

> "In the age of AI, the fundamentals of good product management become even more important, not less."

在AI时代，优秀产品管理的基本原则变得更加重要，而非更不重要。

---

> "In the age of AI, my argument is you need to be founder oriented."

在AI时代，我的观点是你需要以创始人为导向。

## 核心专长

### 1. Product-Market Fit 验证
- 如何识别真实的 PMF 信号
- 避免"虚假指标"陷阱
- 用户访谈和需求验证方法

### 2. Growth Framework
- Weekly Growth Rate 测量
- Retention 作为核心指标
- Growth Loops 设计

### 3. 创业公司失败原因分析
- 最大原因：不与用户交谈
- 过早优化和扩张
- 忽视产品核心价值

### 4. Growth Team 组织
- 何时建立增长团队
- 增长团队的角色和职责
- 跨职能协作模式

---

## 核心观点

> "The most common reason startups fail is simple: they don't talk to users, which means they don't find product-market fit."

> "Weekly growth and retention are the two metrics that make sense for early-stage startups."

> "Watch users do their work rather than asking what they want."

> "Make things people want" — YC 的核心理念

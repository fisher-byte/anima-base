## 核心观点汇总

### 🔑 关键洞察

**关于网络效应**：
> "Network effects are the secret weapon of the most valuable companies in tech. Once you understand how they work, you see them everywhere."

**关于增长渠道**：
> "Over time, all marketing strategies result in shitty clickthrough rates. Growth is about finding fresh powder — new channels where competition hasn't caught up yet."

**关于产品驱动增长**：
> "At Uber, we realized that improving the product — reducing wait times from 10 to 5 minutes — drove more growth than all our marketing combined."

**关于冷启动**：
> "Don't try to boil the ocean. Start with the smallest network that can stand on its own — an atomic network."

**关于指标选择**：
> "Don't pick metrics that make you feel good. Pick metrics that predict whether your users will stick around."

## 核心引用摘录

### 关于客户访谈
> "I did 300 customer interviews before building my startup. Three hundred. More than most companies do in their entire existence. But I made one critical mistake: I was pitching my idea instead of understanding their problem."  
> 
> "我在创业前做了 300 次客户访谈。300 次。比大多数公司整个生命周期做的还多。但我犯了一个致命错误：我在推销我的想法，而不是理解他们的问题。"

### 关于问题方式
> "Don't ask what they want. Ask what they did. Past behavior is the best predictor of future behavior."  
> 
> "不要问他们想要什么，问他们做了什么。过去的行为是未来行为的最佳预测器。"

### 关于透明度
> "Transparency is your moat. Most people are afraid to share their failures, which makes transparency a competitive advantage that's hard to copy."  
> 
> "透明度是你的护城河。大多数人害怕分享失败，这让透明度成为难以复制的竞争优势。"

### 关于场景营销
> "Market to a circumstance, not a demographic. When you describe a specific scenario, your copy feels like you're reading your customer's mind."  
> 
> "针对场景营销，而非人群。当你描述具体场景时，你的文案会让用户觉得你在读懂他们的心。"

### 关于内容创作
> "The best marketing content doesn't feel like marketing. It feels like a friend sharing something useful."  
> 
> "最好的营销内容不像营销，而像朋友分享有用的东西。"

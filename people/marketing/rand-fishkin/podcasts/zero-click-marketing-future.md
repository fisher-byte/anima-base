# Zero-Click Marketing | The Future of Digital Marketing

**节目**: The Next Era of Tech
**嘉宾**: Rand Fishkin (SparkToro CEO)
**日期**: 2025年1月13日

---

## 核心洞察

### 1. 零点击时代已到来

> "Google is now answering almost two-thirds of all queries without a click."

- 60%+ 的 Google 搜索以零点击结束
- 社交平台算法惩罚带链接内容
- AI 工具几乎不发送外部流量

### 2. 营销策略必须改变

**从**：创建内容 → 引流到网站 → 转化
**到**：在用户所在平台创造价值 → 建立品牌 → 自然转化

### 3. 影响力比流量更重要

> "Influence has always been better than traffic. Traffic was always a vanity metric."

- 流量下降不等于业务下降
- 案例：Seer Interactive 流量降 40%，销售涨 20%

### 4. AI 时代的品牌建设

- LLM 不关心链接，关心内容关联
- 品牌需要与相关话题建立语义关联
- 被提及比被链接更重要

---

## 关键语录

> "The social networks where people are spending the majority of their time online... they all have this effect of either completely removing links or penalizing content with links."

> "In our testing, we get about 10 times the reach when we have zero click content versus one that does."

> "Large language models are using content itself. Words that appear frequently near other words."

---

## 应用建议

1. 停止为 UTM 追踪牺牲内容质量
2. 投资长期品牌建设
3. 创建在平台内完整的内容
4. 衡量品牌搜索量而非页面流量

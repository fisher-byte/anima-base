# $46B of hard truths | Ben Horowitz

## Key Insights

### Leadership and Decision Making
> "The worst thing that you do as a leader is you hesitate on the next decision. The thing that causes you to hesitate is both decisions are horrible."

> "The psychological muscle you have to build to be a great leader is to be able to look in the abyss and go, 'Okay, that way's slightly better.'"

> "If everybody agrees with the decision, then you didn't add any value because they would've done that without you. The only value you ever add is when you make a decision that most people don't like."

### Product Management
> "The job is fundamentally a leadership job. And it's a tricky leadership job because nobody is actually reporting to you."

> "It doesn't matter if you write a good spec or you have a good interview. What matters is that the product works."

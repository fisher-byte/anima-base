# Why most product managers are unprepared for the demands of a real startup | Casey Winters

## Key Insights

### Growth Strategy
> "The goal of your Kindle strategies, these like non-scalable hacks, they only exist to unlock the fire strategies, to unlock the things that could take you to millions of users."

### Career Journey
- Started as analyst at apartments.com on marketing side
- Worked on SEO, AdWords, affiliate marketing
- Natural progression to user research and product optimization
- At GrubHub: "we don't care if you work on marketing or change the product, as long as you grow the business"
- Led growth at Pinterest from 40M to 150M MAU

### Product vs Growth
- Growth is a hybrid of marketing and product
- "We now call these roles growth, but that term didn't exist at the time"
- At GrubHub, created product management function out of growth team

## Career Path
- **Apartments.com**: Analyst → Marketing/Product hybrid
- **GrubHub**: 15th employee, led demand side growth from Series A to IPO
- **Pinterest**: Led growth product team (40M → 150M MAU)
- **Greylock Partners**: VC, worked with portfolio companies
- **Eventbrite**: Chief Product Officer (current)

## Companies Advised
Pinterest, Airbnb, Pocket, Eventbrite, Tinder, Thumbtack, Canva
